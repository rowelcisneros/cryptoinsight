  • https://YOUR_GITHUB_USERNAME.github.io/YOUR_REPOSITORY_NAME/
  • https://YOUR_GITHUB_USERNAME.github.io/YOUR_REPOSITORY_NAME/privacy-policy.html
  • https://YOUR_GITHUB_USERNAME.github.io/YOUR_REPOSITORY_NAME/terms-and-conditions.html

Use those public URLs in your Solana dApp Store submission and inside the app listing metadata.
